Hi, it’s nice to meet you!

My name is Meheruz Alam Shishir. I’m a fine-art photographer, I’ve been photographing for almost 5 years. I specialize in making portraits of strangers on the street both digitally and with film. Truth is stranger than fiction, and that fact has always compelled me. I also work as a freelance photography writer for Digital Photo Pro magazine and other industry publications.
